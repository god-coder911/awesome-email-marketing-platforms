## Creator / newsletter platforms

Platforms built for newsletter operators, course creators, podcasters, and paid-newsletter businesses.

- [Kit (formerly ConvertKit)](https://kit.com?utm_source=github-awesome-list&utm_medium=curated) — Creator-focused email platform. Most-distinctive feature: free Newsletter plan up to 10,000 subscribers with unlimited broadcasts — the most generous free tier in this list by a wide margin. Creator Network + subscriber recommendations let existing creators cross-promote to each other's audiences, driving list growth no competitor replicates. 50% recurring affiliate commission (first 12 months) is the highest verified rate in 2026. [Read our comparison →](https://pkpops.com/kit-vs-mailchimp?utm_source=github-awesome-list&utm_medium=curated)

- [Beehiiv](https://www.beehiiv.com?utm_source=github-awesome-list&utm_medium=curated) — Newsletter-first platform built for growth + monetization. Most-distinctive feature: built-in Ad Network that pays publishers for newsletter ad placements + Boosts (paid-growth engine where established newsletters pay to acquire subscribers from smaller ones) — the only platform in this list with native monetization infrastructure built for newsletters specifically. Launch free to 2,500 subs with unlimited sends. Scale $49/mo; Max $109/mo. [Read our comparison →](https://pkpops.com/kit-vs-beehiiv?utm_source=github-awesome-list&utm_medium=curated)

## Ecommerce email + SMS platforms

Platforms with deep Shopify/Magento/BigCommerce integrations, product-event triggers, and predictive analytics.

- [Klaviyo](https://www.klaviyo.com?utm_source=github-awesome-list&utm_medium=curated) — Ecommerce-focused email + SMS + push. Most-distinctive feature: Shopify data model that pulls product-level browse/abandon/purchase events no general-purpose ESP matches — published benchmarks consistently show 2–4× revenue per email on Klaviyo vs Mailchimp for ecommerce. Predictive analytics (CLTV, churn risk) + flows generate 30–45% of total revenue for Shopify brands running Klaviyo properly. Free up to 250 profiles, scales to $720/mo at 50K contacts. [Read our comparison →](https://pkpops.com/klaviyo-vs-mailchimp?utm_source=github-awesome-list&utm_medium=curated)

## General SMB marketing platforms

Mass-market platforms with broad integration ecosystems, landing pages, and all-in-one marketing features.

- [Mailchimp](https://mailchimp.com?utm_source=github-awesome-list&utm_medium=curated) — The classic mass-market email platform by Intuit. Most-distinctive feature: widest integration ecosystem in the category (Zapier, CRMs, ecom platforms, form builders, analytics) — if your tool exists, Mailchimp connects to it natively. Free 250 contacts; Essentials $13/mo; Standard $20/mo; Premium $350/mo. Bills on unsubscribed + duplicate contacts — real cost typically 20–40% above listed. [Read our comparison →](https://pkpops.com/kit-vs-mailchimp?utm_source=github-awesome-list&utm_medium=curated)

## Popup and lead-capture tools

Not senders themselves — pair with an ESP above. Specialized for list-growth via popups, exit intent, and inline forms.

- [OptinMonster](https://optinmonster.com?utm_source=github-awesome-list&utm_medium=curated) — Popup + lead-capture platform by Awesome Motive. Most-distinctive feature: best exit-intent technology in the category — the only lead-capture tool whose exit-intent algorithm correctly detects mobile users (scroll-velocity heuristic instead of cursor-off-viewport). Plays nicely with Kit, Mailchimp, Klaviyo, ActiveCampaign — no email-platform switch required. Basic $7/mo (annual); Growth $49/mo. [Read our comparison →](https://pkpops.com/best-email-marketing-platforms-2026?utm_source=github-awesome-list&utm_medium=curated)
